### Newsletter campaign builder, now you can quickly create awesome newsletters by selecting your post's data and without leaving it or copy paste to third party providers.

== Description ==

Start converting your posts to newletters quickly and easilty with WP Campaigns.

No more copy-pasting a post.
Simply select one of your existing post and its content converts to a newsletter right away!
A set of strict designs for your newsletters keeps your marketing material uniformed,
while editing a newsletter is disabled thus validating end-customers will get the exact content you intended.

The plugin also integrates with Mailchimp with a one-time easy setup.

= Benefits =
No more copy-pasting a post.
Keeps your marketing material uniformed.
End-customers will get the exact content you intended.
Integrates with mailchimp.

= How to use it =
*   Open account in Mailchimp and set private key.
*   Create campaign and set all fields like form, preview text, title, subject line, and replay mail.
*   Select you posts as you want to send.
*   Sent you test.
*   Spread the news.

= Translations =
* English
* Hebrew

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-campaigns.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
